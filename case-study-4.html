Accelerate Sales Order Management and Real-Time Tracking

Home Case Studies Accelerate Sales Order Management and Real-Time Tracking
  • Industry: Medical Solutions
  • Market: Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia, Taiwan, Vietnam, Australia

Challenge

Previously, when an order is generated, it must be manually inputted into SAP. This process entails using an Excel file to monitor the order, its status, and delivery progress. The Excel sheet requires human intervention to extract data from application and update to the SAP. This manual data management increases the likelihood of errors during line-by-line checks, leading to inefficient processes.

Solution

We provide them access to 3 APIs, eliminating manual workflows to enhance application feasibility for patient-centric delivery:

  • Customer Create Sales Quotation
  • Customer Sales Order Get Detail
  • Trace Order Get Status

Business Impact

  • Ensure persistent workflows with automated data integration.
  • Streamline workflows by accessing backend information through calls.
  • Improve resource efficiency and reduce time consumption.
