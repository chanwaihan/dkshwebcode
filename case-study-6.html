API Integration Streamlines SAP Data Retrieval for RPA Processes

  • Industry: Automation
  • Market: Europe

Challenge

The existing RPA processes mimic human actions in retrieving data from the SAP frontend. While automated workflows eliminated the possibility of human errors, they are still susceptible to instability in the SAP environment, particularly when SAP experiences slow loading or freezes.

Solution

We provided access to 2 APIs to streamline the existing RPA processes:

  • Customer Get Sales Order Detail
  • Product Get Product Stock Detail

Business Impact

  • Improved accuracy through SAP backend operations.

  • Streamlined existing RPA workflows.

  • Reduced time for data retrieval from SAP.
